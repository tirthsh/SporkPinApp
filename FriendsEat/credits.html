How to get familiar with our website

  1. Sign in or create an account and sign up.
  2. Once signed in, note the blue marker. This represents your geolocation.
  3. Search a place that you want to see reviews of.
  4. Note the color of the markers. The green markers represents a review done by one of your friends.
    5. Orange marker represents the places your friend's haven't yet reviewed.
  5. Select a marker and you will be able to view the reviews and the respective images of the place.
  6. Click on the Profile tab to see your profile, your friends, and your pending friend requests.
  7. Click on a marker to add a review and let your friends know what you think of that place.
